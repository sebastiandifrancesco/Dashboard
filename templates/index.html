COVID-19 vaccines are more widely available worldwide. In the ever fast-evolving pandemic scenario, it is not simple to identify the countries that are most successfull in making prgress against the fight. In the US, everyone 16 and older is now eligible to receive the vaccine. Yet only about 40% of Americans have received at least one shot and 25% are fully vaccinated.

Our Project Mission

Our dashboard investigates how effective the vaccine is against the virus using data updated in real-time; it contains an interactive Covid-19 heatmap showing every new fully vaccinated person, linecharts over time (days) showing the average number of new confirmed cases, the average number of new deaths, the cumulative new peoples fully vaccinated, and cumulative new deaths. The linecharts contain dropdowns for filtering based on country. There is also a scatterchart that shows global cumulative new peoples fully vaccinated versus global average number of new confirmed cases.
